ABOUT CLOUDSECURE

In cloud systems, misconfigured files are a critical vulnerability, making them prone to cyber attacks that result in data breaches, financial loss, and often both.

Traditional manual detection methods are time-consuming and inefficient. CloudSecure is an AI-powered solution that automatically analyzes configuration files, identifies vulnerabilities, and provides actionable remediation suggestions.

Our convolutional neural network (CNN) model processes your cloud configuration files, highlighting security risks with precision and speed that manual review cannot match.
